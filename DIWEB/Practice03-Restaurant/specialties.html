Elegant Sea and Land

Author: Rob Rose

A masterful symphony of flavors combining succulent, butter-poached lobster tail and a tender, herb-infused beef filet mignon, accompanied by a rich, velvety red wine reduction. This dish is a harmonious union of land and sea, offering a luxurious and unforgettable dining experience.

Woodland Duo Delight

Author: Lois Picken

A sophisticated blend of sautéed wild porcini and chanterelle mushrooms, delicately folded into a luscious spinach and goat cheese quiche, all encased in a light, flaky puff pastry. This dish is a celebration of the earth's bounty, offering a rich and satisfying exploration of woodland flavors.

Fresh Summer Delight

Author: Gary Mundi

A vibrant composition featuring ripe, juicy watermelon cubes paired with tangy feta cheese crumbles, adorned with a drizzle of aged balsamic and garnished with fragrant basil leaves. This refreshing dish captures the essence of a sun-kissed summer day, delivering a delightful burst of flavors with every bite.
