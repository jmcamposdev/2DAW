Air Travel to New Zealand

Traveling to New Zealand by air offers a wide range of options and amenities for international travelers. Direct flights from several major countries and connections through major airlines provide global connectivity to multiple airports in New Zealand, including Auckland, Wellington and Christchurch. Travelers can enjoy modern services, flexible itineraries and a flying experience that ensures comfort and efficiency to reach this stunning destination.

Advantages:

  • Global connections from various airports.

  • Flexibility in schedules and routes.

  • Modern in-flight amenities.

  • Innovative Gastronomy and Exquisite Wines

  • Wide variety of airlines and service options.

Sea voyage to New Zealand

Reaching New Zealand by sea offers a unique perspective for travelers seeking a scenic and relaxing experience. Cruises and ferry services from neighboring countries or via international routes offer immersion in spectacular seascapes and the ability to explore multiple ports of arrival. This option gives travelers the opportunity to enjoy incredible views as they approach the stunning beauty of New Zealand's coastline.

  • Panoramic experience during the crossing.

  • Possibility to explore multiple ports.

  • Modern in-flight amenities.

  • Immersion in spectacular marine landscapes.

  • Opportunity to enjoy a relaxing and scenic cruise.

Main
Airports

Auckland (AKL)

As the country's largest and busiest airport, Auckland International Airport is a major hub. Located in Auckland, New Zealand's largest city, this airport offers a wide range of domestic and international services.

With modern facilities, multiple terminals and numerous airlines operating to and from destinations around the world, it is the main gateway for many travelers visiting the country.

Book Flight

Wellington (WLG)

Located in the capital city of Wellington, Wellington Airport is vital for domestic and international connectivity.

Although smaller compared to Auckland Airport, it offers a variety of services and air connections. As a major gateway to the region, this airport provides convenient access to the city and surrounding areas.

Book Flight

Christchurch (CHC)

Christchurch Airport is the third largest airport in New Zealand and serves as a vital transportation hub in the Canterbury region.

It offers domestic and international flights, connecting travelers to various cities both within the country and abroad. Following the 2011 earthquake, the airport has been renovated and modernized, providing upgraded amenities and services to passengers.
