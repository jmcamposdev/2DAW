Facilities Overview

A World of Convenience
for Everyone

At our Bioparc, we take pride in offering a wide range of amenities designed to make your visit comfortable and memorable. Whether you're a wildlife enthusiast or a curious tourist, you'll find conveniences that cater to your needs.

From picnic areas and children's play zones to restaurants and souvenir shops, our facilities are designed with your comfort in mind. Our commitment is to provide you with an exceptional experience while connecting with nature and enjoying the beauty of our Bioparc.

Additionally, we offer guided tours, educational workshops, and interactive exhibits to enhance your understanding of the natural world. Explore the wonders of biodiversityy and embark on a journey of discovery with our dedicated team of experts.

For those seeking a deeper connection with wildlife, we also provide exclusive behind-the-scenes experiences that offer a glimpse into the daily life of our animal residents and the dedication of our care teams. These unique encounters provide an opportunity to witness conservation efforts up close and personal, leaving you with lasting memories and a profound appreciation for our planet's extraordinary biodiversity .

ACCESSIBILITY AT REAL BIOPARC

Our Commitment to Inclusion

Why Real Bioparc is Fully Accessible?

Here are some of the reasons why Real Bioparc is an accessible place:

  • Wide Paths and Trails: Our Bioparc has wide paths and trails that allow access for wheelchairs and strollers without problems.Interactive and educational exhibits.

  • Adapted Facilities: We have accessible restrooms and other services adapted for visitors with disabilities.

  • Assistance Available: Our staff is trained to offer assistance and guidance to visitors who require it.

  • Rest Areas: We have rest areas and benches distributed throughout the Bioparc so that visitors can relax and enjoy the experience at their own pace.

We are committed to continue improving our facilities and services to ensure that Real Bioparc is an accessible destination for everyone.

If you have any doubts or questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.
